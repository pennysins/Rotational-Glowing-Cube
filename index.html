Rotational Glowing Cube

We came up with this idea because as an I.T, technology is our identity.
We gain attention through the use of technology. Just like the cube's
relentless motion, an I.T is always in motion, constantly adapting to the
ever-evolving technological landscape. The cube represents the drive for progress,
pushing the boundaries of innovation and knowledge. Its movements symbolize the
constant pursuit of improvement and commitment to staying at the forefront of
information technology. The cube's structure, with its clean lines and geometric
precision, mirrors the logical and analytical thinking cultivated in an IT school.
It represents the systematic approach and problem-solving skills that we acquire
during our studies. The cube's sleek design also reflects the emphasis on aesthetics
and user experience in the field of IT, highlighting the importance of combining
functionality with visual appeal. A cube has three dimensions (length, width, and height),
while an IT student explores various dimensions within the field of information technology,
such as programming, networking, databases, cyber security, and more.


That's how we came up with this moving cube animation.
